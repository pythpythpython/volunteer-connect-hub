Schedule Management

Learn how to add events, set reminders, and export to your calendar.

View Guide

Adding Events:

  1. Go to "My Schedule"
  2. Click "Add Event"
  3. Fill in event details
  4. Enable reminders if desired
  5. Click "Save Event"

Exporting:

Click "Export to iCal" to download a .ics file that can be imported into Google Calendar, Outlook, or Apple Calendar.

Hours Tracking

Track your volunteer hours, generate reports, and export data.

View Guide

Logging Hours:

  1. Go to "Track Hours"
  2. Click "Log Hours"
  3. Enter organization, date, hours
  4. Add description of activities
  5. Click "Log Hours"

Tips:

  • Log hours immediately after volunteering
  • Include supervisor name for verification
  • Be specific in your descriptions

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service free?

Yes, VolunteerConnect Hub is completely free to use. It's an open-source project.

Is my data private?

Your data is stored locally in your browser. We don't send your personal information to any servers. Sign-in is optional and uses Google's secure authentication.

Can organizations verify my hours?

Currently, hours are self-reported. Include your supervisor's name when logging hours, and you can generate reports to share with organizations for verification.

How do I report a bug?

Please report bugs on our GitHub Issues page.
